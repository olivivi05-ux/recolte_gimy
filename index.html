About us

Meet the people behind Récolte Gimy.

Récolte Gimy grew from a shared love of fresh food, honest work, and bringing neighbours together. We are proud to offer carefully selected produce and a friendly market experience rooted in our Clarence-Rockland community.

Co-owner

Isabelle

Isabelle brings warmth, creativity, and a keen eye for quality to every part of the market. She loves helping customers discover seasonal favourites and making each visit feel welcoming.

Co-owner

Stephane

Stephane is passionate about fresh produce, dependable service, and building lasting relationships with local growers and customers. He helps keep the market thriving throughout every season.

“Come for the harvest, stay for the people, and leave with something delicious.”
